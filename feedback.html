Feedback Privacy & Rules

Please read all points and make sure you understand them and agree with them fully before submitting the feedback form of the web app Order of Mass.

  • All data is encrypted, so that only the app author can read it
  • Disclosing your e-mail address is not required
    • If you disclose your e-mail, it is used only for a response to the feedback
    • The author of the app does not share your e-mail address with anyone
  • Your IP address is stored in a hashed form, so that nobody (even the app author) can extract the address from the stored value
    • This data is stored to prevent bots/hackers from misusing the feedback form
  • Submitted feedback is not shared with anyone
  • All data is stored at the same server as the website and not being copied/moved anywhere
  • You have the right to request a copy of all your data
  • You have the right to request permanent deletion of all of your data
  • Feedback forms are deleted after one year
  • Cookies are not used in any part of the process
  • In case of any question or request, please use the e-mail tommander@tommander.cz
    • You can use this e-mail address to submit a feedback without using the website form
